Stay Safe Online – Prevention Tips and Cautions

1. Never share your OTP, passwords, or bank details with anyone.
2. Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links.
3. Always verify the website URL before entering sensitive information.
4. Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts.
5. Enable two-factor authentication whenever possible.
6. Do not trust messages claiming urgent action from banks or services.
7. For UPI safety, double-check receiver details before making payments.
8. Do not scan unknown QR codes for receiving money.

If you face any cybercrime, report it at https://cybercrime.gov.in or call the helpline number 1930.
